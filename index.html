About Rupaya

Developing features that serve

We focus on delivering an excellent user experience, innovative features, and near-zero transaction fees.

  • Masternodes process transactions, and owners earn a reward for actively participating in the network.
  • Individuals earn staking rewards, for voting for the most reliable Masternodes.
  • An innovative consensus method called PoSV (Proof of Stake Voting)

Features

The Rupaya blockchain and product ecosystem enable development of high-performance blockchain projects

Fast Blockchain

2000 TPS. 2 Second Blocktime
With a capacity of running 2,000 transactions per second and 2 seconds block time, gone are the long waiting times to get a confirmation

Developer Friendly

EVM Compatible
No hassle of running your own DApp chain. Just port your code from Ethereum and get started with deploying your application within minutes

Security Enhancement

Double Validation and Randomization
In terms of security of consensus protocol, Rupaya proposes Double Validation and Randomization to protect the system

Pay Fee by Token

RupayaZ Protocol
Issue your own RRC21 token in minutes, and let token holders pay transaction fees in the token itself!

Near Zero-Fee

Relying on Masternodes with Proof of Stake Voting (PoSV) consensus
These decentralized nodes can support near-zero fees

Launch a DEX in minutes

RupeX Protocol
A efficient permissionless decentralized exchange (DEX) protocol that empowers a diverse system of DEXs
